Am I crowded with the herd right now? A read on broad-market positioning extremes, sourced from CNN's Fear & Greed composite (market-derived: momentum, breadth, options flow, volatility, credit, and safe-haven demand). Not a stock picker. Just: how stretched is the room.

Reading this honestly: extreme greed has historically been the more actionable side of this gauge, more than extreme fear is — when bulls badly outnumber bears, forward six-month returns have tended to be weaker; when bears badly outnumber bulls, forward returns have tended to be stronger, mechanically, because positioning that's already stretched one way has less fuel left to push further that way. This is a crowding gauge, not a thesis gauge — it says nothing about whether any specific holding's fundamentals are sound.
